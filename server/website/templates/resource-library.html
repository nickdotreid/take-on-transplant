Resource Library

Find medical information, facts, and helpful resources as you consider lung transplant for yourself or as a caregiver.

Topics include: Planning for transplant; caregiver information; transplant referral (including signs that your CF is worsening); eligibility, evaluation, and listing; the transplant surgery; surviving and thriving after transplant; and much more.

This resource can help prepare you for conversations with your CF doctor, your caregivers, and your family/friends.
